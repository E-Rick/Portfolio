Roomly

Roomly is a travel web app focused on helping people find the vacation rental they love. I developed Roomly to create a digital experience and learn new technologies. The app is still under development and I update and share the source code on github.

User Interface

I designed a visual theme that explores modern material design principles and minimalism. Roomly gives users the ability to review rooms, search locations, host listings, and more. I designed a visual theme that is bright and flexible enough for each context within the app.

Branding

When choosing the colors scheme for Roomly, I relied on the the psychology of colors, material design principles, and the anaylsis of competitors, like AirBnb and Hometogo. Roomly wants to help people find a place that they are going to love, and the brand was designed to reflect that. I wanted Roomly to emphasize its purpose as an travel solution, so I incorporated a location pin into my design. To futher add uniquess to the logo, I accented the lowercase 'R' for Roomly.
